Steak

Description:

Chef John's steak frites recipe features a pan-seared and pan-sauced steak, just like you'd get at a French bistro. If you always grill steak, this will be a game-changer. This method delivers a perfectly prepared, perfectly seasoned steak, and can be used with many different cuts beyond New York strip.

Ingredients:

  • 14-oz New York strip steak
  • Kosher salt
  • Black pepper
  • Clarified butter
  • Shallots
  • White wine
  • Beef bone broth

Steps:

  1. Trim the steak.
  2. Refrigerate uncovered for 2-10 hours.
  3. Bring the steak out and sit it at room temperature for 15 minutes.
  4. Preheat a steel pan on high heat.
  5. Cook for two minutes on one side, three on the other.
  6. Add butter and sauce, and serve hot.
